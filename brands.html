Leading German Brands

Logos and short profiles; click to view details.

Mercedes-Benz

Founded 1926. Pioneers of luxury automobiles, safety innovations (ABS, airbags, ESP), and electric mobility with EQ series.

BMW

Founded 1916. Bayerische Motoren Werke. Known for performance, driving dynamics, and the iconic M division. Leader in electric i-series.

Audi

Founded 1909. Four rings symbolize the merger of four brands. Slogan: Vorsprung durch Technik. Innovators in quattro AWD and e-tron EVs.

Porsche

Founded 1931. Ferdinand Porsche's legacy. The 911 is an engineering icon. Taycan leads electric sports car innovation.

Volkswagen

Founded 1937. The 'People's Car' brand. Beetle is one of history's best-sellers. Now leading mass-market EV transition with ID family.

Bosch

Founded 1886. Global leader in automotive components: ABS, ESP, fuel injection, sensors. Also power tools and home appliances.

Siemens

Founded 1847. Diversified engineering giant: energy, automation, healthcare, transportation. Key player in Industry 4.0 and smart infrastructure.

SAP

Founded 1972. World's leading enterprise software company. ERP solutions power global businesses.
