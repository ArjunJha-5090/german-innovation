Automotive Evolution

From Benz (1886) to electric mobility today. Scroll through 137 years of innovation.

1886 – Benz Patent-Motorwagen

Carl Benz patents the first automobile in Mannheim, Germany. The three-wheeled Motorwagen becomes the world's first production automobile.

1901 – Mercedes 35 HP

Daimler-Motoren-Gesellschaft introduces the Mercedes 35 HP, setting new standards for power and speed.

1926 – Daimler-Benz Merger

Daimler and Benz merge to form Daimler-Benz AG, creating the Mercedes-Benz brand.

1938 – Volkswagen Beetle

Ferdinand Porsche designs the Volkswagen Beetle, which becomes one of the best-selling cars of all time.

1963 – Porsche 911 Launch

Porsche unveils the 911, an icon of sports car engineering that remains in production today.

1980 – ABS Technology

Bosch introduces the first electronic Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) for production cars.

2020 – Volkswagen ID.3 & ID.4

Volkswagen's MEB platform brings mass-market electric vehicles to Europe.

2023 – Mercedes EQS

Mercedes-Benz EQS sets new standards for luxury electric sedans with over 700 km range.
